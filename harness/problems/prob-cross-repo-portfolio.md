## Problem
A solo builder running many agents across many projects has no single place that rolls up context,
priorities, and decisions across the whole portfolio — context is siloed per repo/tool.

## Pain
Who: the multi-project solo builder (Ruksak's stated user). How often: whenever prioritising across
projects or context-switching between them. How badly: unclear — plausibly high for a portfolio
operator, but not yet evidenced. Workaround: hold the portfolio view in your head / a separate doc.
