## Opportunity
Capture decisions, alternatives-rejected, and rationale as durable, linked, queryable nodes that
any agent can be pointed at — turning the reasoning trail into reusable context.

## Desired outcome
Past decisions stop being silently re-litigated; agents stop re-proposing rejected options; the
"why" survives across sessions, agents, and (later) teammates.

## Why it's worth it
This is the clearest differentiation from memory/recall incumbents, who store facts not reasoning.
It is also exactly what the harness substrate (decisions/solutions nodes) needs — strategic fit
with the #5 Harness Builder roadmap.

## Open assumptions & risks
- Pain is researched, not yet validated — confirm with user interviews before over-investing.
- Risk of capture friction: if logging a decision is heavy, it won't happen. Must be near-free.
